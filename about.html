About Our Ministry

Learn about our mission, vision, and the work we do in Zambia

Our Story

Go Ye Therefore Zambia was founded in 2010 with a vision to spread the Gospel throughout Zambia and beyond. What began as a small Bible study group has grown into a nationwide ministry impacting thousands of lives each year.

Our Mission

Our mission is to transform lives through the word of God, equipping believers with biblical knowledge and spiritual guidance. We strive to create a community of faith where individuals can grow spiritually, find purpose, and fulfill the Great Commission as instructed by Jesus Christ.

Our Vision

Our vision is to see transformed communities where the love of Christ is evident in every aspect of life, leading to spiritual, social, and economic transformation. We believe that through discipleship and practical ministry, we can see Zambia transformed by the power of the Gospel.

Our Values

  • Biblical Truth: We are committed to the authority and sufficiency of Scripture.
  • Prayer: We believe in the power of prayer as the foundation of all we do.
  • Community: We foster loving relationships that reflect Christ's love.
  • Service: We follow Jesus' example of serving others.
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in all aspects of ministry.
