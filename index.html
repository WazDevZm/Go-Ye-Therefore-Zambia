About Us

Learn more about our mission, vision, and the work we do in Zambia

Our Mission & Vision

Go Ye Therefore Zambia is a ministry dedicated to spreading the Gospel throughout Zambia and beyond. Our mission is to transform lives through the word of God, equipping believers with biblical knowledge and spiritual guidance.

We strive to create a community of faith where individuals can grow spiritually, find purpose, and fulfill the Great Commission as instructed by Jesus Christ: "Go ye therefore, and teach all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Ghost."

Our vision is to see transformed communities where the love of Christ is evident in every aspect of life, leading to spiritual, social, and economic transformation.
